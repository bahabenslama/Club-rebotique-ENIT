ABOUT US

Are you passionate about robotics ?

If so, we have some exciting news for you! We are thrilled to introduce you to the ENIT Robotics Club, an exciting space where your ideas come to life, and where you can develop your robotics skills in a practical and fun way

ENIT robotics club ?

The ENIT Robotics Club is a non-profit organization established in 2008 within the National School of Engineers of Tunis (ENIT) by a group of engineering students who share the same passion for robotics and technological innovation

Why ENIT robotics club ?

Together, we will create, innovate, and leave our mark in the field of robotics. Join the ENIT Robotics Club and discover a world of endless possibilities. We look forward to welcoming you with open arms and seeing your robotic ideas come to life!
